Recommendation System to Increase Sales

Problem description:

Customers often face a large number of product options when shopping online, which can make it difficult to decide what to buy. Many customers may not know about products that match their preferences or needs. A personalized recommendation system uses Machine Learning to analyze customer behavior and suggest products that are most likely to interest them. By studying patterns such as previous purchases, browsing history, and interactions with products, the system can identify relationships between items and customer preferences. For example, if a customer frequently buys technology products, the system may recommend accessories or similar items. This helps customers discover relevant products more easily and encourages them to make additional purchases. Recommendation systems improve the shopping experience by making it more personalized, efficient, and engaging.

Type of Machine Learning:

Unsupervised Learning / Recommendation, because the model identifies patterns and similarities between products and customers without needing explicit labels. In some cases, collaborative filtering or content-based filtering techniques are used to generate recommendations..

Input data:

  • Purchase history of the customer
  • Products viewed or searched
  • Product categories of interest
  • Ratings or reviews given by the customer
  • Frequency of purchases
  • Similar products bought by other customers
  • Time spent viewing specific products
  • Customer preferences and behavior patterns

Expected output:

A personalized list of recommended products for each customer. The recommendations are usually ranked according to relevance or probability of interest, helping the customer quickly find products that match their needs or preferences.

Real-world relevance:

Recommendation systems are widely used in e-commerce platforms, streaming services, and digital marketplaces. They help increase sales by encouraging customers to purchase additional products that match their interests. Businesses benefit from higher customer engagement, improved customer satisfaction, and increased loyalty. At the same time, customers save time because they receive suggestions that are aligned with their preferences, improving their overall shopping experience.
