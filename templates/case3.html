Purchase Probability Prediction

Problem description:

Not all customers who visit a website or store have the same intention to buy a product. Some customers are only exploring options, while others are strongly interested and more likely to complete a purchase. For companies, it is very important to identify which customers have the highest probability of buying, because this allows them to focus their marketing efforts on the right audience. Using Machine Learning, businesses can analyze historical customer behavior and detect patterns that indicate purchase intention. For example, customers who frequently visit product pages, spend more time browsing, or have a history of previous purchases are more likely to buy again. By predicting purchase probability, companies can send personalized promotions, discounts, or recommendations that increase the chances of making a sale.

Type of Machine Learning:

Supervised Learning (Classification), because the model learns from historical labeled data where it is known whether the customer purchased or not. The model then classifies new customers according to their probability of making a purchase.

Input data:

  • Customer age and demographic information
  • Purchase history (previous products bought)
  • Number of visits to the website or store
  • Time spent browsing products
  • Pages viewed or product categories visited
  • Click behavior and interaction with advertisements
  • Frequency of visits within a specific period
  • Customer preferences or interests

Expected output:

A probability value (for example, between 0 and 1, or 0% to 100%) indicating how likely a customer is to purchase a product. The system may also classify customers into categories such as “High probability”, “Medium probability”, or “Low probability” of purchase.

Real-world relevance:

This application is widely used in e-commerce platforms, digital marketing campaigns, and online advertising systems. By identifying customers with high purchase probability, companies can optimize marketing costs, improve conversion rates, and increase sales. It also allows businesses to personalize the user experience, offering products that better match customer interests, which improves customer satisfaction and loyalty.
