Interpretation of Results

🎯 Cluster 0: High-Income Spenders

These customers have high income and high expenses. They represent premium customers with significant purchasing power. Recommended products: Premium credit cards with high limits, investment accounts, and exclusive loyalty programs.

💰 Cluster 1: Moderate Income, Balanced

Middle-income customers with moderate expenses. They show balanced financial behavior with healthy savings potential. Recommended products: Standard credit cards, savings plans, and financial education products.

🏦 Cluster 2: Lower Income, Conservative

Lower income levels with proportional expenses. These customers are more conservative and may benefit from basic financial products. Recommended products: Basic banking accounts, micro-savings plans, and secured credit cards with low limits.

📊 Business Insights

  • The segmentation reveals clear spending patterns that can be targeted with personalized marketing campaigns.
  • Cluster 0 customers should be prioritized for premium product offerings and retention strategies.
  • Cluster 1 represents the largest potential market for cross-selling opportunities.
  • Cluster 2 customers could benefit from financial literacy programs to increase their financial inclusion.
